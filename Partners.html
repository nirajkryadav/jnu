To Partner With Us

This would be a wonderful opportunity for your organization to have some visibility in front of an audience who could benefit and value from your participation at this workshop. We invite you to be a partner with us in this grand event. Advantages of being a Partner with us:

  1. Digital Advertisement – Social Media Campaigns on major platforms such as facebook, twitter, linkedin and instagram.
  2. Branding - Through Hoardings, Banners & Flyers at Jawaharlal Nehru University, New Delhi. Print/Electronic Media - Publicity about the event and partners will be covered by major news channels and leading national newspapers of the region.
  3. Advertisement through FM - Covering an area of 100 kms.
  4. Direct access to our attendees through on site activation as well as leading up to event through brand recognition via various advertising media.
  5. Gaining attention of attendees and students of Jawaharlal Nehru University and converting them into customers.
  6. Win-Win Situation for all partners.
